About Us

A school built for Chopan's children

S.N.G Public School (Shardhanand Goswami Public School) is located in Gaurav Nagar, Chopan, in Sonbhadra district of Uttar Pradesh. We offer education from Nursery to Class 12th, with a focus on discipline, strong fundamentals, and all-round development for every child in our town.

We believe a good school isn't judged only by results — it's judged by how ready its students are for the world outside the classroom. That's the standard we hold ourselves to, every single day.

For admission enquiries, call +91 94555 65405 or message us on WhatsApp — our office will guide you through the process and required documents.

Academics

Nursery to Class 12th

Nursery – Class II

Foundation years focused on language, numbers, and good habits through friendly, activity-based teaching.

Class III – V

Building strong basics in Hindi, English, Maths, and EVS with regular practice and attention.

Class VI – VIII

Science, Social Science, Maths, and languages deepen, with computer basics introduced.

Class IX – X

Full board-pattern preparation across all core subjects, with regular tests and doubt sessions.

Class XI – XII

Stream-based teaching (Science / Commerce / Arts) with focused board exam preparation.

Sports & Activities

Daily physical activity, plus yearly sports events and cultural programs for every age group.
